## 🔬 Engineering Vision: Hard-Wired AI & Edge Architecture
</div>

Software Engineering student at UE Potsdam. My engineering focus lies in descending from high-level LLM abstractions down to the bare metal. I am actively researching **Edge AI and custom silicon-level accelerators**, moving away from general-purpose GPUs toward hyper-efficient, ASIC/FPGA-based logic. My R&D spans from architecting RISC-V/ARM logic to deploying decentralized **Autonomous Command & Control (C2) Mesh Networks** where intelligence is processed at the source, not in the cloud.
